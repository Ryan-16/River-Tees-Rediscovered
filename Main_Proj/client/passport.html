How To Use

  • Use the arrows on either side of the screen to move between pages
  • To skip to a trail or sculpture, either click on its name on the Contents page or click on its icon on the Map page
  • Use the arrows at the top of the page to jump between trails
  • Use the link in the top left corner to return to the Contents page at any time
  • Use the link in the top right corner to return to the Map page at any time
  • To upload your own photo of a sculpture, hover above its photo in the passport then click 'Upload'
  • When you upload one or more photos of a sculpture, arrows will appear below the default photo, allowing you to browse through all photos of that sculpture
